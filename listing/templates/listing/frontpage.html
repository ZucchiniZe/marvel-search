Welcome to nightcrawler!

Nightcrawler is a indexing and searching site for marvel unlimited. This was made out of frustration of the listing and searching of the marvel site. It is a very simple webscraper backed by a postgresql database for a cache. The name nightcrawler was inspired by the comic book character nightcrawler since this website scrapes marvel.com.
